About Quarantutors


WHO WE ARE:

A community of high school students that recognize the devastation caused by COVID-19 and seek to bridge the gap in resources by providing extra academic assistance.

OUR STORY:

The idea for Quarantutors arose when we noticed that the kids who we once tutored were not going to have the resources necessary to be successful in school while in quarantine.After realizing the demand for extra homework help, we reached out to our local community and built a team. Our team of tutors is passionate, driven, and dedicated to providing valuable assistance to your child.


Our Values

MISSION:

Quarantutors aims to bridge the digital divide by offering virtual homework help during the COVID-19 crisis because many students do not have the resources at home to complete homework accurately.

SERVICES

How it works:

At Quarantutors, we offer K-8th online homework help Monday - Friday in 30 minute sessions. You decide the times and we will match you with one of our tutors!
